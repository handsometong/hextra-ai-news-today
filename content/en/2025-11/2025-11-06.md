> `AI News` | `Daily Briefing` | `Tools` | `Research` | `Industry` | `Open Source` | `Human Impact`

- Beijing plans an **AI Hospital**, Tesla’s Optimus training crew wears 40-pound rigs to record motion, and publishers fight Google’s AI Overview siphoning traffic.
